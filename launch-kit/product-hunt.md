## Full description

You don't need a document-automation SaaS to make a quote with a total at the bottom.

Quotewell is a fast, private, desktop-first quote & estimate builder:

- **Price catalog** — save your services once with default price, unit, and description, then build quotes in minutes instead of retyping the same line items forever.
- **Real quote builder** — quantities, per-line discounts (flat or %), quote-level discount, tax, live totals computed in exact integer cents. Optional/alternate lines show the upsell without inflating the total.
- **Branded PDF export** — your logo, your accent color, your terms boilerplate. One click.
- **Quote history that sells** — duplicate a past quote for a similar job, mark quotes won or lost, and see your open pipeline value at a glance.
- **Yours forever** — everything is a local SQLite file. Works offline. No account. No telemetry. MIT-licensed source on GitHub.

PandaDoc and Proposify gate quoting behind $19–$49 per user per month. Quotewell is **$29 once** — it pays for itself in about six weeks and then it's free for the rest of your business's life.

## Maker first comment

Hey PH 👋

I built Quotewell because I got tired of paying $19/mo (per user!) for what is, honestly, a form with line items and a total at the bottom. Every "quoting tool" I tried wanted to be a sales-enablement platform — CRM sync, e-sign workflows, analytics dashboards — when all I needed was: pick services from my price list, set quantities, apply a discount, add tax, export a PDF that looks professional, and remember whether the client said yes.

So that's exactly what Quotewell is. It's a desktop app. Your data is a SQLite file on your own machine. The money math is integer cents all the way down, so totals are always exact. The source is MIT on GitHub — you can build it yourself for free, or grab the $29 packaged installer if you value your afternoon.

Ask me anything — especially if you run a service business and quote weekly. I want to know what your quoting workflow actually looks like.
